My travels


Disney World

A fun theme park with lots of rides to enjoy. It has many "worlds" and has butiful decorations around holday time. It also has many food consetions so you won't be hungry. Be sure not to get confused with this one and the Disney land in California!



Cincinati Zoo

See lots of animals and have lots of snacks! There's even a merrygoround and a train ride! They have just about evey animal you can think of!



Newport Aquarium

There are tons of sea creatures at this aquaruim, even penguins! My favorite was the overhead/all around veiw of the animals. It's a very fun place to visit!



Cincinati Childrens Museum

There are so many fun places to visit in this museum. They have a couple of wonderful exibits, a very fun play center for kids, and a oximax theater, which is always playing some kind informational, but intresting movie. Denfinitly a fun place to visit.



St. Louis Arch

This place has everything you need to have fun. They have two mini exibits, a train ride to the top of the arch, where you can see around for probably miles, a ferry ride, with 3 layers and a helpicopter ride! Oh, and don't forget, there ar snacks as well! Definitly a very, very fun and cool place to visit!
